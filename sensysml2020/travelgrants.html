Registration and Travel Grants

Registration for SenSys-ML 2020 is through CPS-IoT Week.

SenSys-ML 2020 welcomes applications for travel grants. Keep the following in mind:

  • Travel grants are intended to partially cover your expenses related to attending and/or presenting at SenSys-ML 2020.
  • The exact number of grants and their amount will be determined after the applications are finalized.
  • You will have to pay for travel ahead of time and apply for reimbursement (with receipts) after the conference.
